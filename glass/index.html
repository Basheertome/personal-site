Google Glass

Interaction Research

While working as an interaction researcher on Glass, my job was to play with experimental hardware and software input technologies, in order to come up with new user-centric application concepts for them. I then used a range of design and prototyping methods to inspire and rally people around these ideas.

During my time there, I worked closely with industrial designers, engineers, user-experience designers, and high-level managers to help affect the strategic planning and future development of the product.
