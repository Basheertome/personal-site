Hi, I'm Basheer
. I'm a hardware interface designer cozying up to a calm, warm future with cooking & technology.

Through a blend of electronics prototyping, filmmaking tools, and physical form finding techniques, I create new hardware interfaces that seamlessly fuse digital interfaces with analog methods of interaction.

By night, I write about kitchen tools through the lens of design and technology over at C100. By day, I'm a design lead at Google working from end-to-end to craft hardware products like the Daydream Controller.

More often than not, you'll find me pointing my camera directly into the sun, hoarding imagery for inspiration, or rewatching Lurpak butter commercials.

Get in touch on Twitter, Instagram, or Email.

I also keep up an archive of my work history and my student projects.
